Project Description

Vaccination Site Queueing System is a mobile application that was created at the heat of the Covid-19 Virus. This application allows its user to provide the needed information at a vaccination site while maintaining contactless approach to the people on site. It is also used by the staff in the site who are responsible for the vaccination proper, this will allow them to know people's information without having to do any physical interview eliminating physical contact.
