Project Description

Online Barangay Portal is a generic web app built in consideration of Barangay information and transaction management. This app targets both the officials and residents of the Barangay as its users. The system will allow the barangay officials to do administrative and management chores while allowing the residents to access resources and information that they need. Combined with proper implementation and application, this app will provide ease and convenience for each and every one of its users.

Built with:

PHP, SQL, Javascript, HTML, CSS
